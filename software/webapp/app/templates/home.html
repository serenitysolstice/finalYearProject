The aim of this project is to create a collection of scientists that lived and worked in Wales over a century long period. This collection has been built from the work already done by the National Library of Wales to collect and digitize newspapers that were published between 1804 and 1919.

This collection may be both incomplete and incorrect in places - due to the nature of automated information extraction, the results you see here will not be perfect. However, this is a working project, and attempts will continue to make this collection a worthy tribute to a rich history of science in Wales.
